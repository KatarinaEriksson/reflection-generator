The purpose of group reflections:
- Express thoughts, feelings and opinions about a shared experience
- Build openness and trust
- Draw out key learnings and insights to use in subsequent experiences
Reflecting involves considering, thinking about and exploring what the event:
- meant to me
- how I relate to what has happened
- what I experienced and how I feel about it
How
- Step 1. Take some time to reflect on your own
- Step 2. Sit in a circle where everyone can see each other
- Step 3. Use the Reflection Generator Timer to make sure everyone has an equal amount of sharing-time
- Step 4. Share your reflections and insights
Guidelines for reflections
- Speak from your individual perspective (“I-statements”)
- Avoid generalizations (e.g. “everybody” and “some people”)
- This is not a debate! Don’t interrupt others when they are sharing.
- Practice active listening when others are sharing their reflections (+ länk http://toolbox.hyperisland.com/active-listening ) Individual reflection?
